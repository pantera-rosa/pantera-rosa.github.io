FUSER Data

Context

The FUSER dataset consists of 8 types of CSV files, each providing various flight and airport-related data. The scheduled arrival throughput (TBFM) is based on the scheduled times of arrivals, while the estimated arrival throughput (TFM) is derived from real-time updates to estimated arrival times. Another important feature is the estimated departure throughput (ETD), as a high number of departures can indicate a higher number of arrivals in the future. Additional features include counts for aircraft type, carrier, and engine class (MFS), which are related to factors such as resilience to adverse weather and the time required for takeoff. The runway-to-gate time (CONFIG) provides insights into the level of ground traffic activity, and the local weather forecast (LAMP), with a one-hour resolution, includes nine weather conditions, such as wind and precipitation, that could impact operations.
