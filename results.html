Results

Model Evaluation

Due to the nature of our model (regression based analysis), the metric utilized to measure its performance is its Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) calculated as exp(-RMSE/K), where K is a normalization factor of 10, and exp is the exponential function in order for it to be a number between 0.0 and 1.0, with 1.0 representing a perfect score (RMSE = 0). As of 12/6/2024, our model scored 0.72.

Feature Importance

We performed a permutation based analysis to determine which features were most important. Based on our analysis, the following features were selected:

  1. estimated_arrival_thoroughput
  2. hour_sin
  3. run_way_departure_count
  4. hour_cos
  5. airport_5
  6. ETD_departure_count
  7. airport_1
  8. airport_6
  9. airport_0
  10. airport_9
