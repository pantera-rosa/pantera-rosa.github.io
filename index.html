About

I am a Technical Policy Advisor & Professional Staff Member on the Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Committee in the United States Congress. In a past life, I built software at NerdWallet, Google, and a few startups. I have a Master's degree in Data Science from UC Berkeley and an interdisciplinary Bachelor's degree in technology and art from the University of Southern California.

I am a data obsessive, I like bicycles, and to my mother's dismay, I dedicate about 7% of my daily caloric consumption to Dino Nuggets. I enjoy teaching and asking questions.
